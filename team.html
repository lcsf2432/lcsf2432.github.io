BlendNest's story

2017: The spark of an Idea

You know those late-night conversations where big ideas are born? That's how BlendNest started. Rachel Adams, our founder, wanted to create kitchen tools that weren't just functional but also beautiful—something that made cooking feel like an experience, not a chore. With a small team and a big dream, BlendNest came to life in a cozy home office.

2021: Expanding the nest

By 2021, we realized one thing: you wanted more. So, we expanded our collection to include terracotta measuring cups, glass storage containers, and other thoughtfully designed tools. Every product stayed true to our promise—beautiful, durable, and kind to the planet.

2024: Going Global

This year, we've gone global! BlendNest is now in kitchens across 20 countries (and counting). It's surreal to think that what started as a small idea has grown into something that reaches homes around the world.

2018: The first collection

Fast forward to 2018, when we launched our first product: the Sage Green Mixing Bowl Set. It wasn't just a hit—it became a favorite for home cooks who wanted style and functionality in one package. Seeing the joy our bowls brought to kitchens fueled us to keep going.

2023: connecting through food

2023 was all about connection. We launched our first cooking workshops, bringing people together to share recipes, stories, and laughs. It was amazing to see how much magic happens when people gather in the kitchen—and we felt so lucky to be part of it.

Today: The Journey Continues

Here we are today, still growing, still dreaming, and still making kitchen tools we're proud of. We couldn't have done it without you—our community of home chefs, bakers, and food enthusiasts. Thank you for letting BlendNest be part of your story. Let's keep mixing joy into every meal together.
