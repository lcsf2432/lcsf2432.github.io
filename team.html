BlendNest's story

2017: The beginning with small idea

With so many late night planning for perfect designs and brand design, our Founder Rachel Adams decided to launch first product in 2017. We started with basic product like Stainless Steel Mixing Bowls, Glass Mixing Bowls. We gathered feedbacks and did few surveys to improve our product. So, it was a small start with big dream.

2021: Expanding Aesthetic Color Collection

By 2021, we realized one thing: You love neutral colors as much as we do. So, we added more colors to collection. We added Charcoal, Olive, Sand, Light Grey colors to our collection. Along with these, we added wooden bowls.

2024: Going International

This year, we've gone International! BlendNest is now in kitchens across US and Canada. It's incredible that we started as a small idea on paper about our brand design and now we are spreading our wings into States.

2018: The first aesthetics collection

Fast forward to 2018, we launched our very first Aesthetic Mixing Bowls collection including calm neutral colors and pastel colors. We included Beige, Ivory, and Taupe colors. We received incredible response and immense love for our collection.

2023: Training and spreading awareness

In 2023, we decided to start virtual workshops using our products. So, you can create different recipes at you home. By this, we could guide you how to maximize use of our products. We started with free workshops at first. Later on we collaborated with popular chefs to do workshop for popular dishes. It's paid one.

Today: The Journey Continues

Here we are today, still dreaming, still thinking, still improving, still growing. We are so proud of our journey and we could not make it without you. Thank you for letting BlendNest be part of your kitchen!
